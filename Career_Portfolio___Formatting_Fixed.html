What is Trust & Safety?

Every time you scroll through a social feed and don't see graphic violence, scam ads, or hate speech aimed at your community, someone made that happen. That's Trust & Safety.

At a technical level, it's a system of policies, classification frameworks, quality measurement, and AI calibration that determines what billions of people do and don't see online. We build the taxonomy of what violates a platform's rules. We measure whether enforcement is accurate. We identify where models are getting it wrong and why. We train the AI to do better.

At a human level, it's the responsibility of protecting real people from real harm while making sure we're not silencing legitimate expression. Every decision sits on that line. Getting it right takes analytical precision, cultural fluency, ethical judgment, and the ability to make fast calls under pressure with imperfect information.

It's the kind of work where you need to understand both the data and the people behind it.
